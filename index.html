What keeps you up at night

Three worries every family has — and what we do about them.

Worry 01

Is something slowly getting worse?

Our sensors learn your loved one's normal — wake times, kitchen visits, movement patterns — over the first 2 to 4 weeks. After that, we tell you when things start to drift. The gradual changes a weekly phone call would never catch.

Worry 02

What if they fall and nobody knows?

Ceiling-mounted FP400 radar detects falls in seconds, including in the bathroom where most happen. Posture detection means we can tell the difference between your mother lying on the floor and lying on the couch.

Worry 03

What if something goes wrong in the house?

AC failure detection, indoor temperature alerts, and water leak monitoring — built specifically for Phoenix summers, where an AC failure can become an emergency in hours.

Common questions

What families ask before saying yes.

How does the service agreement work?
You have two options. Pay the full equipment cost upfront and stay flexible — your monthly service is on its own terms. Or choose a 24-month service agreement with reduced installation cost up front. About 80% of our families choose the agreement. We'll walk through both in the consultation and help you pick what makes sense.
What if my loved one is outside the home?
Our in-home sensors cover the house. For everywhere else, the Belle+ pendant (included with Complete Care) extends protection to anywhere with cellular coverage. It has GPS, two-way voice with a trained operator, automatic fall detection, and a 30-day battery. It's waterproof and wearable in the shower.
What happens if my loved one falls?
Inside the home, our ceiling-mounted FP400 radar detects falls in seconds — no button to press, no wearable required. Posture detection means it can tell "lying on the floor" from "lying on the couch." On Complete Care, the Belle+ pendant adds automatic fall detection for outside the home, with two-way voice to the monitoring center so help can be dispatched to their exact GPS location.
Is anyone watching cameras inside the home?
No. We don't install cameras anywhere. Our radar sensors detect presence and posture, not images. There is no video feed of your loved one at any point, ever.
What if my mom doesn't want to wear anything?
She doesn't have to. The Smart Home and Smart Home + Monitoring plans require no wearables. The pendant is only included with Complete Care, and even then it's optional — many families choose to leave it on the bedside table for outings.
How long does installation take?
Under two hours. No drilling, no wires, no disruption. Our installer will walk through the home with you to confirm the layout, then place everything where it makes sense.
Do we get charged for adding family members?
No. Unlimited family logins on every plan. Adult children, siblings, spouses, anyone you want — same view, same alerts.
