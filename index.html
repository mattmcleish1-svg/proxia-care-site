Common questions

The things families ask us first.

Are there cameras in the house?
No. Ever. Our sensors notice patterns — movement, temperature, sleep rhythms — without watching or recording anyone. Nothing sees her, nothing films her, and nothing ends up on the internet. It's the difference between a home that quietly notices and a home under surveillance — and it's why parents actually say yes.
How does this compare to the cost of assisted living?
Assisted living in the Phoenix area typically runs $4,000–$8,000 a month, and the average stay is about two years — a six-figure decision. Proxia costs a small fraction of that. Most families tell us the move happened not because their parent suddenly needed nursing care, but because nobody could be sure she was okay at home. That certainty is exactly what we provide — and we'll give you exact pricing for her home at the free assessment.
What if she won't wear a pendant?
It happens — and it's why the home layer matters. The sensors work whether or not the pendant is on her wrist, so the family still gets the weekly report and the safety alerts even on the days it sits on the nightstand. Most parents come around once they realize the pendant is theirs, not a leash.
Is there a contract?
Yes — 36 months, because keeping your parent home is a long-term plan, and we price it like one: we install everything, we own the equipment, and we service it forever at no extra cost. And in writing: if she ever moves to full-time care, or passes, the agreement ends. Nobody pays for service they don't need.
Who responds if something happens?
A professional, UL-listed emergency monitoring center staffed 24/7 by trained operators — the same caliber of response center that protects hospitals and businesses. They speak with her through the pendant, dispatch help if needed, and notify the family immediately.
What happens at the free assessment?
The founder visits in person — with you and your parent together — walks the home, points out what he sees (good and bad), and recommends the right plan for her specific home. About 45 minutes, completely free, no obligation. If Proxia isn't the right fit, we'll say so.
