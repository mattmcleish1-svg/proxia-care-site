Common Questions

What Phoenix families ask.

Will my loved one have to wear anything?

The mobile pendant is small, lightweight, and waterproof — most seniors forget they're wearing it. Inside the home, our radar sensors work without any wearable at all. Your loved one doesn't need to interact with any technology.

Are there cameras in the home?

No cameras anywhere. Our system uses millimeter-wave radar — the same technology in modern automotive safety systems — to detect presence, posture, and falls without recording any video or audio.

What happens when my loved one needs help?

If they press the pendant button or a fall is detected, they're instantly connected to a trained operator through the pendant's two-way speaker. The operator can talk to them, locate them via GPS, notify your family, and dispatch emergency services. For in-home sensor alerts (heat, activity anomalies), your family is notified by email and SMS within seconds.

What if the internet goes out?

The in-home hub continues processing locally during internet outages. The mobile pendant works independently on its own 4G cellular connection — it doesn't need wifi or internet at all.

What if my loved one isn't comfortable with technology?

That's the point. The sensors are invisible — they don't interact with them at all. The pendant is one button. There are no apps to learn, no screens to check, no devices to charge except the pendant once a month. The technology works around their life, not the other way around.
